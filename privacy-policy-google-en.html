Privacy Policy

App "Sostoyanie"

The "Sostoyanie" app respects your privacy and does not collect personal data from users without their consent.

Collected Data

  • The app may use the device's local storage (SharedPreferences) to save your settings and records.

Advertising

The app contains ads provided by third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob or Yandex Ads). These services may use cookies and device identifiers to show personalized ads.

App "Tarot"

The "Tarot" app is an elegant and intuitive tarot reading application designed to provide users with a mystical and immersive experience.

Collected Data

  • The app uses the device's local storage to save your settings and reading history.
  • For advertising, anonymized data about your device and location may be collected.
  • No personal data is collected or shared with third parties.

Functionality

  • Intuitive Spreads: Fully functional 3, 5, and 10-card spreads for comprehensive readings.
  • Free Access: All tarot spreads are available without subscription, making divination accessible to everyone.
  • Visual Excellence: Beautiful gradient backgrounds, redesigned cards with stylish shadows, and improved typography.
  • Seamless Navigation: Smooth transitions between screens and an intuitive bottom navigation bar for easy exploration.

Advertising

The app contains ads from third-party services. These services may use cookies and device identifiers to show personalized ads.
