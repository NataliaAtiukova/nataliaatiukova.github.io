NutriAI

NutriAI is an innovative iOS application designed to help users track calories, monitor activities, and achieve fitness goals with ease. Built using Swift, SwiftUI, SwiftData, and HealthKit, NutriAI incorporates AI-powered insights to enhance daily nutrition and fitness routines.

  • Daily Calorie Tracking: Visualize calorie consumption, track macronutrient distribution, and receive personalized diet recommendations.
  • Activity Tracking: Log activities like walking, running, and cycling with a built-in timer, calculate calories burned, and save session histories.
  • HealthKit Integration: Sync data like active energy burned, steps, exercise minutes, and sleep analysis from Apple devices.
  • Personalized Meal Plans: AI-powered meal plans tailored to dietary goals, featuring diverse options like balanced diets or plant-based meals.
  • Goal Setting: Set daily calorie targets and monitor progress to stay on track with your fitness journey.
